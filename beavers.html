BEAVERS...for fun!

5-7 years

Meets every Thursday 18H15-19H30

Beaver scouts are the youngest members of the scouting family. Starting at the age of 5 they join a Colony and follow a programme based on their motto ‘Making Friends’. Beaver scouts have a promise but not a scout law. Instead, they are introduced to the concepts of scouting through a varied programme which includes four challenges, namely ‘Discovery’, ‘Friendship’, ‘Creativity’ and ‘Outdoor’. These challenges are completed thanks to many activities during outings and weekly meetings. Beaver scouts enjoy making things, singing, playing games, investigating nature, going out on visits, storytelling, learning how to be safe and most importantly making friends!
