ROVERS...for life!

18-26 years

Meets on request

The fifth and final section of the Scouting family is the Rover crew which is made up of Rover scouts aged between 18 and 16. The Rover programme is aptly entitled ‘My Journey’ as the time spent with the crew is indeed a journey which opens doors to new friendships, fun, adventure and unforgettable experiences through which Rovers are able to develop their leadership and organisational skills. The Rover programme takes into account the fact that most individuals at this age are about to embark on a new adventure called ‘real life’ and is flexible allowing individuals to complete as much as they wish at a time convenient to them. The Crew becomes a support system where Rovers can strengthen friendships, giveback to the community, grow as individuals and overall enjoy life.
